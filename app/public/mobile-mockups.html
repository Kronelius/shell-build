Mobile Mockups Catalog — Other Surfaces

Companion to /mobile-contacts-mockup.html. Six additional patterns covering every other flagged page in SHELL_MOBILE_RESPONSIVE.md: Invoices list, Reminders inbox, Team members, Roles & Permissions, Invoice line items (modal), and Messaging (list + thread). All built to fit a 375px viewport with zero horizontal scroll.

Theme: Rainier Facility Solutions (navy). · Patterns map 1:1 to the §3 canonical recipes in the handoff doc.
